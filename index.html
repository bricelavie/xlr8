Privacy Policy

Personal Data

xlr8 requests permission to access the device's GPS location to perform speed and distance calculations. This location data is not collected and is only stored for the duration of the app's runtime.

xlr8 shows AdMob Google Inc. (Google Inc., 1600 Amphitheatre Parkway, Mountain View, CA 94043, USA) advertisements. AdMob usually uses personal data to provide safe, relevant and discreet advertisements. The collected personal data can be but is not limited to your device ID and location data as well as the “Apple Advertising Identifier” of iOS and the “Android Advertising ID” of Android (which are used to create an anonymous user profile). The collection of the personal data is covered by article 6 (1) (f) of the GDPR as it is needed to finance the development of the GPS Race Timer app and to provide it to you free of charge. In your device settings you can find options to limit the usage the advertising ID for advertising. However GPS Race Timer only requests non-personalized advertisements from Google by enabling the restricted data processing for all shown advertisements. Additional information about the restricted data processing can be found here: https://support.google.com/admob/answer/9561022. Please see this information page https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads and the AdMob privacy policy: https://www.google.com/intl/en/policies/privacy/ to find out more details.

By using the app you agree to the handling and processing of your personal data as described.
